COLOR PALETTES (Intimate Warmth Progression)
=============================================

story progresses — like dimming lights as you get closer to someone.

TYPOGRAPHY
==========

Google Fonts (via next/font/google):
- Playfair Display — headings, chapter names, stat callouts
- DM Sans — body, labels, captions
- Dancing Script — emotional moments only, used sparingly
  (love letter, end card, one or two pull quotes)

Background Particles:
- Tiny petals or soft dots drifting upward on all slides
- Color matches current act accent
- 15-20% opacity, never distracting
- Density: low (20-30 particles max)

TRANSITIONS
===========

Background Color:
- Smooth 1.2s transition between act shifts
- No hard cuts

Act Title Pages:
- Full-screen title card before each new act
- 1.5s fade-in
- Press → to continue to first slide of act

Slide Transitions:
- Fade + 20px upward drift
- 0.5s ease
- All elements animate on slide enter, not page load
